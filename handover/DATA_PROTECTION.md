## 1. Lawful Basis for Processing

### 1.1 Legal Basis
Data processing under this service is conducted under the following lawful bases:

1. **Consent**: Users explicitly consent by dialing the USSD code and providing information
2. **Public Task**: Ward administration functions as a public authority
3. **Legitimate Interests**: Efficient constituent services and issue resolution

### 1.2 Applicable Laws
- Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - for EU citizens
- Constitution of Kenya - Right to Privacy (Article 31)

---

## 2. Data Types Collected

### 2.1 Personal Information
| Data Type | Purpose | Storage Duration | Legal Basis |
|-----------|---------|------------------|-------------|
| Phone Number | Identification & communication | 12 months | Consent |
| Full Name | Constituent registration | 12 months | Consent |
| National ID / Huduma No. | Identity verification | 12 months | Public Task |

| Issue Description | Service request handling | 12 months | Consent |
| Bursary Application Data | Education support processing | 24 months | Consent |

### 2.2 Technical Data (Non-Personal)
- Session IDs (temporary, deleted after session)
- Service Code dialed
- Timestamp of interaction
- Response times (aggregated, anonymized)

### 2.3 Data NOT Collected
- GPS/location tracking
- Device identifiers (IMEI, etc.)
- Third-party app data
- Biometric information
- Financial/payment information

## 3. Purpose of Data Collection

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
1. **Constituent Registration**: Maintain registry of ward residents
2. **Issue Reporting**: Track and resolve constituent concerns
3. **Bursary Management**: Process education support applications
4. **Announcements**: Communicate ward updates and events
5. **Project Tracking**: Inform residents of development projects

### 3.2 Secondary Purposes (with explicit consent)
- Statistical analysis (anonymized)
- Service improvement (aggregated metrics)
- Compliance with legal obligations

|---------------|------------------|---------|

## 6. Data Subject Rights

### 6.1 Your Rights Under Kenya DPA & GDPR
1. **Right to Access**: Request copy of your personal data
2. **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate information
3. **Right to Erasure** ("Right to be Forgotten"): Delete your data
4. **Right to Restriction**: Limit processing of your data
5. **Right to Data Portability**: Receive data in machine-readable format
6. **Right to Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
7. **Right to Withdraw Consent**: At any time

### 6.2 How to Exercise Rights

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Technical Measures
- **Encryption in Transit**: TLS 1.2+ for all connections
- **Encryption at Rest**: AES-256 for database storage
- **Access Control**: Role-based access (MCA, PA, technical staff)
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication for administrators
- **Logging**: Comprehensive audit trails (who, what, when)

### 7.2 Organizational Measures
- **Staff Training**: Annual data protection training
- **Confidentiality Agreements**: Signed by all staff
- **Incident Response Plan**: 72-hour breach notification protocol
- **Regular Audits**: Quarterly security reviews

### 7.3 Data Breach Protocol
In the event of a data breach:
1. **Immediate containment** (< 1 hour)
2. **Assessment** (< 24 hours)
3. **Notification to Data Commissioner** (< 72 hours)
4. **Notification to affected users** (< 72 hours)
5. **Remediation and post-incident review**

## 8. Cookies & Tracking

## Appendix A: Definitions

- **Personal Data**: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
- **Data Controller**: Entity determining purposes and means of processing (Kyamatu Ward)
- **Data Processor**: Entity processing data on behalf of controller (Render.com, MongoDB)
- **Data Subject**: Individual whose personal data is processed
- **Processing**: Any operation performed on personal data
- **Consent**: Freely given, specific, informed indication of wishes
